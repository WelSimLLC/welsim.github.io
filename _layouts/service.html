Services

WelSim is committed to providing the best software development services to our distinguished clients.

WelSimulation LLC (WelSim) provides a wide range of engineering simulation service specializing in product re-invention and optimization, computer-aided analysis, enterprise application software development, and simulation-oriented training and education. At WelSim, we appreciate that every clientele is unique. We understand that what might be right for one client is not necessarily right for all others, and we dedicate to every single project to our client's specific demand.

Privacy and discretion are our top priorities. We have strict protocols in managing contracts and securing client's data. All of our work are conducted in the United States, no outsourcing/subcontracting occurs without an agreement with our customer. Further, the information of clients and projects will not be released to any third-party agency.

Services

Our services include but not limited to:

    Enterprise-grade software development.
    Numerical methods and applications, e.g. finite element method, finite volume method, spectral methods, continuous and discontinuous Galerkin methods, molecular dynamics, etc.
    Consulting for scientific computing and engineering simulation subjects, e.g. computational solid mechanics, computational electromagnetics (CEM), computational fluid dynamics (CFD), etc.
