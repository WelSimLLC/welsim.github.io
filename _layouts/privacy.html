Privacy Policy

WelSimulation LLC (“WelSim,” “us,” “we”) protects any information that can be used to identify you directly or indirectly (“Personal Data”). This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how “WelSim collects and processes Personal Data on the www.welsim.com website and any other WelSim websites on which this Policy appears (“WelSim Ecosystem”). By accessing, viewing, browsing, and otherwise using the WelSim Ecosystem, you agree with this Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Policy, you should not access, view, browse and otherwise use the WelSim Ecosystem.

I Have a Question About This Policy; How Can I Reach WelSim?

You may direct all questions and concerns about this Policy or questions regarding exercising your privacy rights to WelSimulation LLC, 140 Mayview Rd. Ste. 208, Bridgeville, PA 15017, United States, a data controller for personal information collected and processed in connection with the WelSim Ecosystem. You may contact WelSimulation LLC via regular mail or email at info@welsim.com.

What Information Do We Collect and How Do We Collect It?

(1) Information you may provide to us:

You may share information about you by corresponding with us, or filling in forms in the WelSim Ecosystem, including information you provide when reporting a problem with the WelSim Ecosystem, participating in the WelSim beta testing program, or by corresponding with us by phone, email or otherwise.

(2) Information we collect about you:

We automatically log information about you and your computer when you visit the WelSim Ecosystem, as explained in more detail below. This includes computer operating system type,‎ browser type, and language, how long you spent on a page,‎ access times,‎ geolocation, and information about your use of and actions on the WelSim Ecosystem.

(a) Aggregate Data:

As is true of most websites, WelSim gathers certain information automatically and stores it in log files. This information includes aggregated data such as internet protocol (IP) address, operating system, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit website pages, date/time stamp, clickstream data, and wallet services metrics (including your interactions with service features and content, your preferences, and occurrence of technical errors). We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the WelSim Ecosystem, to track users’ movements around the WelSim Ecosystem and to gather demographic information about our user base. We do not link this automatically collected data to personally identifiable information. We may disclose aggregated information about you and information that does not identify any individual or device, without restriction.

(b) Tracking Technologies

WelSim Ecosystem uses cookies. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive by a website. Cookies help WelSim to make our WelSim Ecosystem and your visit better. We may also use cookies to see which parts of the WelSim Ecosystem people use and like, and to count visits. We process the cookie data collected in accordance with this Policy. You may, at any time, permanently deny the setting of cookies by changing the setting of your Internet browser. This is possible in all popular Internet browsers. However, deactivating the setting of cookies may change the availability of some functions available through the WelSim Ecosystem.

You can learn more about how cookies work at: https://allaboutcookies.org/.

What Legal Basis Do We Have to Collect Your Information?

WelSim collects Personal Data only when we have a legal basis to do so.

In most cases, and where processing of Personal Data does not unduly infringe your rights and freedoms, WelSim relies on our legitimate interests in conducting normal business operations. Our legitimate interests include but are not limited to, enhancing the security of our network and information systems, carrying out processing operations for statistical purposes, and identifying or preventing fraud.

We may need to process your Personal Data in order to negotiate or perform on a contract to which you are a party.

In some cases, we may have to process your Personal Data to comply with laws and regulations.

Does WelSim Share My Personal Data?

WelSim is not in the business of selling your Personal Data. However, your Personal Data may be shared with others in accordance with this Policy in the following circumstances:

(a) Third-party service providers

Third-party providers employed by WelSim to perform functions on our behalf may require access to Personal Data to perform their services. Such functions may include data analysis, payment processing, content transmission, etc. However, all third-party providers must process Personal Data in accordance with this Policy and as permitted by applicable data protection laws.

(b) Protection of WelSim and others

WelSim may release Personal Data to comply with the law or with regulatory obligations; enforce or apply our Terms of Use and other agreements; protect WelSim’s rights, property, and safety; and protect the rights, property, and safety of others.

How Secure is My Personal Data?

WelSim Ecosystem is designed with security in mind. We use encryption protocols and software to protect your Personal Data during transmission, and work to maintain electronic, physical, and procedural safeguards when collecting, storing, or disclosing your Personal Data.

Can I Withdraw My Consent for WelSim’s Processing of My Personal Data?

If you consented to WelSim’s processing of your Personal Data for a specified purpose, you may withdraw your consent at any time. Subject to applicable law, you have the right to request access to, correct, and delete your personal data, and to ask for data portability. You may also object to our processing of your personal data or ask that we restrict the processing of your personal data in certain instances. If you wish to do any of these things, please contact info@welsim.com.

How Long Does WelSim Keep My Personal Data?

We keep your personal information to enable your continued operation of the WelSim Ecosystem, for as long as it is required in order to fulfill the relevant purposes described in this Policy, or as may be required by law such as for tax and accounting purposes, or as otherwise communicated to you.
